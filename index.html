Understanding Your Mind

MindMirror is a cognitive assessment platform designed to help you understand and track your mental performance. In our digital age, excessive screen time and information overload can impact our cognitive abilities without us realizing it.

Through interactive assessments and an AI-powered mental health assistant, we help you monitor your reading speed, working memory, and overall cognitive wellness. Whether you're a student, professional, or anyone curious about digital wellness, MindMirror offers a simple way to check in with your mind and learn about your cognitive patterns.

Digit Span Test

Test your working memory capacity

Numbers will appear on the screen one by one. Remember them in order and type them back when prompted. The digit span test measures short-term memory and attention capacity.

  1. Click "Start Test" to begin
  2. Watch the numbers appear one at a time
  3. After all numbers are shown, type them in the same order
  4. The test gets progressively harder with more digits
  5. You get 2 attempts per level
